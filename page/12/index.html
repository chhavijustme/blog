Have you been ghosting Helvetica?

The movie, Helvetica embodies me as a powerful ubiquitous spirit as if I really exist. How can a typeface be somebody, anybody in this case? Well look around, you probably interact with me just as much as you do to anybody. You could have just ambled past the wall covered with tons of posters or gazed up longingly at the brands you would like to own.
